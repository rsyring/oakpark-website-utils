theDispersion

theDisperson is for students 6th - 12th grades with games and hanging out, followed by a verse by verse lesson and age/gender divided small group discussion.

We meet each Wednesday night from 6:30-8:00 following the Greater Clark County School schedule from August to May.

Student Discipleship Classes are 12 weeks long emphasizing memorization and teaching of various Christian topics to educate your student on the Bible and how to apply it to their world.

Check out the church calendar for other events including retreats, summer camp, and community service days.
