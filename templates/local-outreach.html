North American Mission Board

Oak Park partners with the North American Mission Board to plant churches, send missions teams, and provide disaster relief around Southern Indiana and the United States.

For more information, visit their website.

Louisville Rescue Mission

Oak Park partners with Louisville Rescue Mission on all our benevolence and individual emergency relief. Founded in 1881, Louisville Rescue Mission exists to proclaim the gospel and extend God's mercy to the homeless and hurting of the greater Louisville area in support of the local church. They embrace men and women experiencing homelessness and poverty with the compassion of Jesus Christ through a variety of programs - including emergency services, residential LifeChange program, and permanent housing.

For more information, visit their website.

Choices Life Resource Center

Choices Life Resource Center is a Christ-centered, life-affirming pregnancy care center offering compassion and direction to pregnant and parenting clients. Services include pregnancy testing, limited OB ultrasound, options counseling, post-abortion healing Bible study, parenting education and material support, as well as STD/STI testing and treatment.

For more information, visit their website.
