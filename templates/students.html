Introduction

Oak Park's student ministry for Middle School and High School is located above the gym in our youth friendly multipurpose room. We are one ministry, but separate the two age groups as often as possible. Our goal is to teach your students the Bible in-depth using language they can comprehend. We also work to give parents the resources and training to lead their home in Christian faith.
