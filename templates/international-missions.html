International Mission Board

Oak Park partners with the International Mission Board to send missionaries around the globe. Short term, long term, and carrer missionaries are sent in a varriety of contexts.

For more information, visit their website.

The Haiti Collective

Oak Park partners with the Haiti Collective who seeks to connect healthy stateside churches with local Haitian congregations for gospel ministry. Oak Park has been in partnership with a church in L'estere, Haiti since 2012, aiding with orphan care, pastoral education, and church development. If you're interested in joining one of our mission teams to Haiti contact Pastor Chase Sears.

For more information, visit their website.

Teaching Truth International

Oak Park partners with Teaching Truth International to equip pastors and resource churches in areas where theological and practical training is unavailable.

For more information, visit their website.
